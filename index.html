Gioi thieu san pham

Giai phap ho tro toan dien cho nguoi khiem thi trong boi canh su dung thuc te tai Viet Nam

Artificial Eyes duoc xay dung voi muc tieu ket hop nhieu tinh nang von thuong tach roi tren thi truong vao cung mot he thong. San pham gom ung dung di dong, phan cung ESP32 va co che dieu khien bang giong noi de giam thao tac tay cho nguoi dung.

Thiet bi huong den ba bai toan chinh: phat hien vat can som de tranh va cham, giup doc van ban bang OCR ket hop Text-to-Speech va giu ket noi khan cap voi nguoi than khi xay ra su co te nga.

Giao dien va luong su dung duoc thiet ke de nguoi dung co the kich hoat chuc nang bang giong noi.

Tinh nang cot loi

7 tinh nang duoc tich hop trong cung mot he thong

Thay vi tach rieng nhieu thiet bi ho tro, Artificial Eyes tap trung cac nhu cau thiet yeu cua nguoi khiem thi vao mot quy trinh su dung thong nhat, uu tien giong noi va phan hoi bang am thanh.

Canh bao chuong ngai vat

Camera va AI phat hien vat can hoac nguoi xuat hien tren huong di, giup nguoi dung chu dong tranh va cham.

Do khoang cach bang sieu am

Cam bien HC-SR04 do khoang cach vat can trong tam 20 - 150 cm va gui canh bao khi xuong duoi nguong an toan.

Doc van ban bang OCR offline

Dien thoai chup anh van ban, nhan dang noi dung bang OCR va doc lai bang giong noi de nguoi dung tiep can thong tin nhanh hon.

Nhan dien nguoi o gan

He thong co the nhan dien nguoi than hoac nguoi xung quanh de ho tro di chuyen va tang cam giac an tam khi su dung.

Tro chuyen cung AI

Nguoi dung dat cau hoi bang giong noi, he thong xu ly noi dung va phat lai cau tra loi de ho tro hoi dap nhanh.

Nen tang phat trien

Phan mem di dong

Ung dung duoc phat trien tren MIT App Inventor, dam nhan viec ket noi Bluetooth/Wi-Fi, xu ly OCR, Text-to-Speech, chatbot AI, thoi gian - thoi tiet va giao tiep khan cap.

Phan cung nhung

ESP32 dong vai tro dieu khien trung tam, nhan du lieu tu 4 cam bien HC-SR04, phoi hop voi servo, pin 18650 va cac tin hieu am thanh hoac rung de dua ra canh bao.

Thanh phan chinh

Dieu khien & ket noi

ESP32, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, dien thoai thong minh co camera, GPS, loa va micro.

Cam bien & nguon
